SUMMARY

BOTH HOUSE AND TECHNO ARE DIVIDED IN A TOTAL OF 49 SUBGENRES, SORTED BY YEAR OF ORIGIN AND MUSICAL INTENSITY. MUSICAL INTENSITY IS BASED ON MULTIPLE FACTORS LIKE BPM, RHYTHM, INSTRUMENTS USED, PRESENCE OF VOCALS, ENERGY AND PITCH. THESE ATTRIBUTES ARE COLLECTED BY THE ECHO NEST API. FOR EARCH OF THE SPECIFIED SUB-GENRES, A FIXED AMOUNT OF SONGS WITH A TOP RATING IS USED TO DETERMINE THE INTENSITY COEFFICIENT.

HOVERING OVER A SUB-GENRE HIGHLIGHTS THE NAME IN THE RIGHT TOP CORNER AND SELECTING A SUB-GENRE HIGHLIGHTS RELATED GENRES. DOUBLE CLICKING A SUB-GENRE LEADS TO THE ARTISTS PAGE IN WHICH ARTIST INFORMATION ABOUT THE SPECIFIED SUB-GENRE CAN BE FOUND.
