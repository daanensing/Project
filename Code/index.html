INTRODUCTION

WHILE HOUSE AND TECHNO MUSIC ARE BECOMING MORE POPULAR, THE DIFFERENTIATION BETWEEN SUB-GENRES OF THESE ELECTRONICAL MUSIC GENRES AND THE ORIGIN OF THESE MUSICAL GENRES BECOMES MORE AND MORE VAGUE. THIS PROJECTS OFFERS AN INSIGHT IN DIFFERENT SUB-GENRES OF THESE MUSICAL GENRES. BESIDES SERVING AS A TOOL TO DISCOVER ALL GENRES, ONE CAN DISCOVER ARTISTS AND MUSIC CORRESPONDING TO THESE GENRES.

ADDITIONAL INFO

THE MAIN VISUALIZATION CONSISTS OF A SCATTERPLOT IN WHICH 49 SUB-GENRES OF BOTH HOUSE AND TECHNO MUSIC ARE BEING SHOWED BY MEANS OF A SCATTERPLOT. FOR ANY SUB-GENRE, AN OVERVIEW OF TOP ARTISTS AND RELATED ARTISTS CAN BE FOUND BY CLICKING THE SUB-GENRE. SUBSEQUENTLY, A NEW VISUALISATION IS SHOWN BASED ON THESE ARTISTS.

RESOURCES

FOR A START, DATA FOR THE MAIN VISUALISATION IS COLLECTED BY USE OF SEVERAL BOOKS AND ONLINE RESOURCES. SOME MIGHT ARGUE THAT SUB-GENRES ARE MISSING OR ON THE OTHER HAND BE OVERSTATED IN THIS VISUALIIZATION. THE ULTIMATE GOAL HOWEVER IS TO PROVIDE AN INSTRUMENT TO GAIN INSIGHT IN THESE MUSICAL CATEGORIES WHICH IS AS ACCURATE AS POSSIBLE. A COMPLETE OVERVIEW OF BOTH ONLINE RESOURCES AS LITERATURE USED FOR SETTING UP THE DATASET CAN BE FOUND HERE.
